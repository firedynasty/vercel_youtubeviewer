**Spinach** is extremely versatile and nutrient-dense:

**Lettuce** is great for:

- Wraps instead of tortillas
- Adding crunch to sandwiches
- Using as "cups" for taco fillings

**Celery** works well as:
- A snack with hummus or peanut butter
- A crunchy addition to tuna or chicken salad
- A flavor base for soups and stews
- A crisp addition to stir-fries

**Carrots** offer versatility:
- Raw snacking with dip
- Roasted as a side dish with olive oil and herbs
- Grated into salads or slaws
- Added to soups and stews

All of these vegetables keep reasonably well in the fridge, making them practical choices for regular consumption. Even adding just one or two of these foods a few times a week could help boost your nitrate intake and potentially support your mitochondrial health.
